Hi, I am a Senior Researcher at Isomorphic Labs, where I develop AI to revolutionise drug discovery. I previously worked at Google DeepMind on problems at the intersection of ML, physics and chemistry.

My research interests include generative modelling, learning simulation and enhanced sampling with large-scale applications in statistical mechanics and fluid dynamics.
